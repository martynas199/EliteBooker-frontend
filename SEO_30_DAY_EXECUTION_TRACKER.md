# 30-Day SEO Execution Tracker (UK Booking Software)

## Goal

Increase qualified organic traffic and conversions for UK high-intent queries around booking software, pricing, and platform comparisons.

## Primary KPI Targets (30 Days)

- Non-brand clicks (Search Console): +20%
- Top 20 keyword count for money/comparison pages: +30%
- CTR on comparison pages: +1.5pp
- Demo/signup conversion rate from organic landing pages: +15%

## Priority URLs

- Home: /
- Pricing: /pricing
- Compare hub: /compare
- Compare pages: /compare/vs-fresha, /compare/vs-treatwell
- UK money pages:
  - /salon-booking-software-uk
  - /barbershop-booking-software-uk
  - /nail-salon-booking-software-uk
  - /beauty-salon-booking-system-uk
  - /hairdresser-booking-software-uk

## Week-by-Week Plan

### Week 1 — Indexation + SERP Baseline

- [ ] Submit sitemap in GSC and confirm fetch success
- [ ] Validate index status for all priority URLs
- [ ] Export baseline for clicks, impressions, CTR, avg position
- [ ] Run `npm run seo:snapshot` and archive `reports/seo-priority-snapshot-YYYY-MM-DD.csv`
- [ ] Verify canonical consistency for priority URLs
- [ ] Confirm noindex pages are excluded as intended

### Week 2 — On-Page Refinement

- [ ] Refresh title/description copy on underperforming pages (low CTR)
- [ ] Add 3-5 internal links from high-traffic pages to comparison pages
- [ ] Expand FAQs on comparison pages based on real search queries
- [ ] Add 1 proof element (case study/review) to pricing and compare pages
- [ ] Update Last Updated date on comparison pages after any edits

### Week 3 — Authority + Distribution

- [ ] Publish 2 supporting blog posts that link to money pages
- [ ] Acquire 5-10 relevant backlinks (directories, partner mentions, PR)
- [ ] Share comparison pages in owned channels (newsletter/social/community)
- [ ] Reach out to 10 UK niche communities for mention opportunities
- [ ] Track referral traffic to compare and pricing pages

### Week 4 — Conversion Optimization

- [ ] Review organic landing-page conversion funnel
- [ ] Test one CTA variant on landing and compare pages
- [ ] Test one hero message variant on /pricing
- [ ] Improve page sections with highest drop-off from analytics
- [ ] Final 30-day KPI review and next-cycle roadmap
